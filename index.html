Black Lives Matter Movement

This story map disscusses the conversation of the BLM movement on Twitter and dicusses geo based trends.

Black Lives Matter

The map to the right portrays the tweets that are in favor and or supportive of the black lives matter movement and were found and collected by using a twitter api to scan through tweets with the key words/statement "Black Lives Matter" and BLM/blm.

Opposition

The map to the right portrays the tweets that are against and or lean to the "All Lives Matter" stand point. While all lives certainly do matter, the current and apparent need to abolish systemic racism of the world and the opression of the black communities and people of color takes priority. This section shows the regional trends of tweets involving "all lives matter" and other oppositional key words. These tweets were also found and collected by using a twitter api to scan through tweets with the key words/statement such as "All Lives Matter".

About

This is a story map that was created as the final project for GEOG 458: Advanced Digital Geographies at the University of Washington, Seattle. Spring Quarter 2020. This story map was designed to bring about the conversation of regional trending of those for the Black Lives Matter movement versus fascists, those opposed, and or anyone 'ignorant' to the situation, by using geo located tweets. This map also delves into an analysis on the connection between so those for or against the movement, their respective locations, and whether or not they correlate to the party majority of the home state of location. The story map library is derived from the storymap plugin for leaflet at http://atlefren.github.io/storymap/, and its functionality relies on both leaflet and Boostrap.
